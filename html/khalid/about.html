About Al Hilal Hotels chain

More than a decade of excellence in hospitality service

Our story

The story of Al Hilal Hotels began in 2010 with an ambitious vision to offer a unique hospitality experience that blends modern luxury with authentic Arabian hospitality. Since then, the chain has grown to become one of the leading hotel chains in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We are proud to serve four major cities: Makkah, Madinah, Jeddah, and Riyadh. Each of our hotels is carefully designed to reflect the character of its host city while maintaining the standards of quality and luxury that distinguish our brand. Our dedicated team works around the clock to ensure that every guest has an unforgettable experience. We are committed to providing the best service and the highest levels of comfort for our valued guests.
