About Hilal Hotels Chain

Over a decade of excellence in hospitality service

Our Story

The story of Hilal Hotels Chain began in 2010 with an ambitious vision to provide a unique hospitality experience that combines modern luxury with authentic Arabic hospitality. Since then, the chain has grown to become one of the most prominent hotel chains in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We are proud to provide our services in four major cities: Makkah, Madinah, Jeddah, and Riyadh. Each of our hotels has been carefully designed to reflect the character of the host city while maintaining the quality and luxury standards that distinguish our brand.

Our dedicated team works around the clock to ensure that every guest has an unforgettable experience. We are committed to providing the best services and highest levels of comfort to our valued guests.
